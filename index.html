About CorpGovData

CorpGovData is Canada's emerging platform for accessing comprehensive publicly available corporate and government information. Our mission is to promote transparency, accountability, and informed decision-making by providing easy access to public data sources across Canada.

As a centralized hub for public information, we aim to serve researchers, journalists, businesses, legal professionals, and citizens who require reliable access to corporate registries, government filings, and other publicly disclosed information. Our platform will aggregate data from federal, provincial, and territorial sources, making it easier than ever to access the information you need.

Our Vision

We envision a Canada where public information is truly accessible to all. By leveraging modern technology and user-centered design, CorpGovData will break down barriers to information access and empower Canadians with the data they need to make informed decisions.
